Educators

Improve the guidance of learners

The PSET CLOUD platform bridges the gap between all
players in the post-school education and training worlds.
It enables educators to guide learners towards
opportunities and to offer guidance informed by data.

Learners

Quickly match with opportunities

A big problem is that learners often pursue studies in
saturated fields and then struggle to find employment.
PSET CLOUD helps learners to see where the gaps are
and what opportunities would be best to pursue.

Employers

Experience greater efficiency and
reduced costs

The PSET CLOUD platform offers valuable and accurate
data that empowers informed decision making and future
planning. This is a collaborative tool that helps to connect
key players.
